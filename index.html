About

We are a starting data science consulting company

We started Lisbon Dynamics with the purpose of developing challenging and innovative Data Science projects. Each of the founders has more than 18 years of experience in multinational consulting and technology firms, having successfully served several industries over the years. Fueled by the dynamics of our brand name, we assume the challenge of finding innovative and differentiated solutions through our experience and professionalism, agility, creativity and a good degree… of “boldness”.

From Lisbon to the World!

We are a newly started company! We retained the past professional experiences and incorporated a new, truly entrepreneurial culture, compatible with the people we want to have with us, where talent and the passion for excellence are of primary importance. We have, at its inception, the ambition and the goal of only getting involved in truly challenging projects. Be it by innovation or size. We guarantee individual coaching and development of each element of the team.
